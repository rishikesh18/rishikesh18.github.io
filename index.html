Full Stack Web Developer:

I worked as web developer for four years. I mostly worked with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and SQL and recently I completed a Certification for Full Stack Web Development through The Friday Center at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill for modern web development frameworks. Through this certification, I gained experience with the MERN Stack and developed various full stack applications. I learned several other technologies after completion of my certification course of Full Stack Development. I have experience in HTML5, CSS3, PHP, JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Express.js, React, Redux, React Native, Angular, Node.js, Database Theory, MongoDB, MySQL, Firebase, Heroku, Git, C#, ASP.Net and various other leading-edge technologies. I love to learn new technologies and develop new applications. I love playing sports, hiking and travelling. I played cricket for my colleges (Tri-Chandra college and Tribhuvan University, Nepal) and currently playing triangle cricket league for Cary Warriors in North Carolina, US.
