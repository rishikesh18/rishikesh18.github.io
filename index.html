About Me

I am a student of full stack web develpor at UNC at Chapel Hill coding boot camp. I did my high school in science faculty from Kathmandu BernHardt College, Nepal. I did my bachelor's degree in meteorology from Tri-Chandra Campus (Tribhuvan University), Kathmandu, Nepal and did my master's degree in meteorology from Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu, Nepal

I worked as a hydrologist in Recham Consult, Nepal for two years and worked as assistant meteorologist for government of Nepal for four years.

I spend my childhood in Syangja, a beautiful hilly district of western Nepal. After completing 10th grade, I left my village for higer education. I lived in Kathmandu for 7 years for my higher education. In 2013, after getting selected in assistant meteorolgist position through tough competion through public service commission of Nepal, I was posted in Okhaldhunga, a remote eastern hilly region of Nepal. I spent my four years working there.

I have great interest in cricket. I played lot of games while I was in college. I represented Tri-chandra Campus and Tribuvan University teams in various games. My team become champaions for two time in intra college cricket of Tri-chandra college and one time in inter department cricket tournament of Tribhuvan University

My hobbies inculeds hiking, cycling, swiming and visiting remote places of Nepal. I have visited 66 districts of Nepal out of 75. I have hiked the most beautiful trekking routes of Nepal which includes Annapurna Basecamp, Annapurna cricuit, Tilicho lake, Kanchanjanga route, Renjo La and Gokyo trek of Everest region, and many more. I have been to 18790 feet above mean sea level.
